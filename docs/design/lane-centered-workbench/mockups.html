Design artifact · primary UI reference

Lane-Centered Workbench

These mockups capture the desired product hierarchy, density, terminology, and tone. They are intentionally more specific than the prose proposal: attention first, action second, provenance on demand.

How to read these mockups

The goal is not a glossy AI dashboard. The goal is a serious operating surface where humans and agents share one project reality. The UI should look ordinary enough to ship: tables, scoped signals, clear next actions, and status explanations that say why the system believes what it believes.

# RFC 10155: Modes of Collaboration

- **Supersedes**: RFC 0004


## Summary

Define distinct "Modes" (Thinking Partner, Chief of Staff, Maker) for the AI agent depending on the phase of work.

## Motivation

Instead of rigid "personas", we need flexible modes that align with the natural lifecycle of a project phase (Plan -> Manage -> Execute).

## Detailed Design

### 1. The Thinking Partner (Architect Mode)

**Focus**: Exploration, Tensions, "Why".
**When to use**: Phase Planning, Design Reviews.
**Mindset**: Surface tensions, challenge assumptions, align with axioms.

### 2. The Chief of Staff (Manager Mode)

**Focus**: Organization, Cadence, "What".
**When to use**: Phase Transitions, Context Restoration.
**Mindset**: Context is King, coherence, obligations.

### 3. The Maker (Implementer Mode)

**Focus**: Execution, Efficiency, "How".
**When to use**: Implementation, Coding.
**Mindset**: Follow the plan, bounded rationality, incremental updates.

## Alternatives

**Single Persona**:

- Pros: Simpler.
- Cons: The agent gets confused about whether it should be creative or compliant.

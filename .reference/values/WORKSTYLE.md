# Workstyle and Modes of Collaboration

This file captures how I prefer to work with AI in this exosuit: thinking partner, chief of staff, and everything in between.

It is intentionally fuzzy to start and should be refined as we notice what actually works.

## 1. Preferred Modes (Outline)

- Thinking partner: exploring ideas, tensions, and tradeoffs
- Chief of staff: organizing, summarizing, and keeping me on track

## 2. Depth vs. Breadth (Outline)

- When we favor deep dives vs. broad passes
- How the AI should check which mode we are in

## 3. Questions vs. Guesses (Outline)

- When to ask clarifying questions instead of guessing
- When it is acceptable to make a best-effort guess and move forward

## 4. Feedback Loops (Outline)

- How my feedback on drafts and structures should shape future behavior
- How often we should reflect on whether our collaboration style still fits

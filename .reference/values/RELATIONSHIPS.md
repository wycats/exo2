# Relationships and Ongoing Context

This file captures how we want to think about relationships (work and personal) in this exosuit, and what kind of context we want to preserve.

This is a fuzzy first draft, intended to be refined once we have a better feel for what is actually helpful.

## 1. Purpose and Scope (Outline)

- What belongs in relationship context here vs. in other systems
- The goal of relationship tracking (e.g. context for obligations, empathy, long-term arcs)

## 2. Relationship Files or Sections (Outline)

- Possible structure for per-person or per-group notes
- What kind of information we keep (history, current dynamics, open loops)

## 3. Cadence of Review (Outline)

- How often we look at relationship notes
- How AI can suggest revisiting relationships based on obligations or calendar events

## 4. Privacy and Sensitivity (Outline)

- What we intentionally do *not* record
- How we talk about sensitive topics in a way that still feels safe

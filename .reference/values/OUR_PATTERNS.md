# Our Patterns

"Our patterns" is the name we give to the shared cognitive patterns that emerge between Yehuda and Exo in this exosuit.

They are more specific than high-level values and more stable than one-off prompts. They influence Exo's personality and default behaviors.

This file is a sketch of what we mean by patterns and how they evolve.

## 1. What Patterns Are (Outline)

- Reusable ways of thinking or acting that show up across contexts
- Often compressed into short handles or phrases
- Grounded in Yehuda's writing and our lived collaboration

## 2. Relationship to Values and Files (Outline)

- How patterns relate to `values/` files
- How patterns might be referenced from other documents (e.g. "per our pattern X")

## 3. Creating and Updating Patterns (Outline)

- How new patterns are discovered (e.g. after resolving a tension repeatedly)
- When we decide to name a pattern explicitly
- How we retire or revise patterns that no longer fit

## 4. Patterns as Personality (Outline)

- How a stable set of patterns shapes Exo's "feel" in this repository
- How patterns might be ported (carefully) to other environments without pulling in this entire repo
